OUR ROOTS

"I just couldn't stop climbing..."

GYM-CEPTION is a gym inside of a gym inside of a gym. The overarching gym is a climbing gym, but we also have a dojo for Taekwondo, and inside the dojo is a cross fit double dutch gym.

Founded by friends who are passionate about a few things: climbing, outdoors, the environment, climbing mountains, indoor climbing gyms, saunas, dogs, each other, community, sunsets, sunrises, snow, sauna, and so much more. We're so stoked you're thinking about becoming part of this journey. Or just lost on this site. Whatever is clever duder!

Some of our founders haven't stopped climbing since they started. Instead of walking, they climb horizontally around from place to place to make sure they keep up appearances.
