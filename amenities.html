AMENITIES

DOG PARK

Did you know climbers are 98% more likely to have a dog? Of which 50% are australian shepards, and another 25% are Chihuahuas? We did our research to make sure the amenities climbers care about are taken care of!

Rock-In Gym has a dog park on premise so you don't have to be anxious about your furry child getting anxious at home (or in your van). No more torn couches or car seats thanks to our non-supervised dog park!

TAEKWONDO

Climbing takes focus and concentration, and so does martial arts. We've partnered with our local Taekwondo friends to bring you the best of both worlds. Rank up your belts while you rank up your climbing skills!

SPA

We're not monkeying around when we tell you we have the finest spa in town. Not only do we have a luxury UP imported sauna, but we also have geothermal pools that run into our facility and provide luxurious healing waters filled with all of the essential vitamins and nutrients known to science.

If you need pollen, the Pollen Man will get you everything you need.

DOUBLE DUTCH

We have gyms inside of gyms inside of gyms. We like to call it the gym-ception. That's really what we should be called, but our founders didn't think of the name until writing this paragraph. Rock-in Gym was a literal play on having rock inside of a gym...

Not only do we have a climbing gym, and taekwondo dojo, but we also have a crossfit gym. We reuse all of our old ropes and hand stitch them together to make sure they're nice and thick. We then have people use them for double dutch... which is a great work out!
