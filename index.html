WHY GYM-CEPTION?

SAUANA

Most climbing gyms think people join the gyms to climb, but we recognize and understand that there's so much more to climbing than being up on a big wall. Not only are people going way up on our walls, but the temperature is going way up in our authentic wood fired barrel saunas imported from the Upper Peninsula of Michigan.

We go out of our way to make each experience within our facility as real as possible. Each day we import snow from the local ice rink shavings and place them amongst low light conifers to make it feel like you're actually wherever you imagine cold-piney-sauna-filled-places to be. Maybe it's somewhere in Northern Europe, or Northern Japan? Don't forget about way south like Argentina! Brr! But don't forget, you'll be hot in the sauna.

COMMUNITY

People joke about paying for friends, but when you join GYM-CEPTION, that's exactly what you're doing. No judgement zone here! We love when people pay for their friends, because that means there's more people up on the walls, and let's be honest... we're a business. We need to make money along the way. So this is our way of saying we appreciate you giving us money to come hang out with your friends. We won't talk about how much money it'll cost, because that would be bad business. We want you to think about it, and lead you on, then when you get to the membership page we'll tell you how much it is. If you join our newsletter and give us some of your info we might sweeten the deal. We'll see!

Community nights are our thing! We welcome all people, and hope you'll enjoy our community as much as we do. Not just because we're making money off them, but we really think all (well most) of our members are pretty sweet.
